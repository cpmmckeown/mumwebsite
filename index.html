About

Give back tours combine the best of an authentic experience in the country of your choice with an immersive and adventurous holiday. All of the tours listed on this site are run by local, independent operators and all involve a 3-day volunteering experience. Each of these trips have been vetted and offer a way to offer valuable assistance whether it’s a beach clean, turtle conservation, planting of native trees, caring for elephants, building frames for sloth bears or taking part in a building project to provide essential services for families in need.

Sheila Fitzgerald

Retired but loving travel, I want to help you get in touch with tour operators around the world.

  • Email: email@example.com

Creating a web page with travel information was about the last thing I ever envisaged myself doing. Having travelled on organised giveback adventures in 2018 + 2019, the tour operator folded in 2020. As a qualified money advisor in a previous role, I set up a support group to help people get money back and it seems to have helped. It wasn’t long before people got the travel bug again but as the local tour operators who had worked with the bankrupt company were left high and dry with one of their main referral sources gone to the wall, it seemed like the perfect opportunity to intervene.
